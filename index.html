Caluwe

Journal

On my first day as a software engineer at a new company, I expect to feel a mix of excitement and a healthy dose of nerves. I see myself arriving at the office early, full of energy and curious about what the day will bring. The office will be modern, with open workspaces and technology all around me.

I will be greeted by my manager or a colleague and guided to my workstation. My first impression will be a warm welcome, with friendly and helpful colleagues. I expect an introductory session where I will learn about the company culture and the main projects.

Throughout the day, I will meet my immediate team members and possibly other teams with whom I will collaborate. I will log into the systems, such as the development environment and communication tools, and my manager will give me an overview of the ongoing projects and my first tasks.

Tips for myself:

- Make a good first impression.

- Ask questions to show that you are interested in what the company does.

- Actively listen to colleagues and team leaders to understand what is required.

- Take notes of important terms and questions to further research at home.

- Be positive and open to create good relationships.
